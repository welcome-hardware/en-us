What Is the Trézor Hardware Wallet?

The Trézor Hardware Wallet is a physical device that stores your private keys offline, safeguarding them from hackers and malware. Unlike software wallets, which are vulnerable to cyberattacks, Trézor provides an extra layer of security by keeping your keys completely offline.
